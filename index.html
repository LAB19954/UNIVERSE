Darlingg,

Yeh khat likhna mujhe nahi aata — matlab seriously, main itna eloquent nahi hoon. Par tujhse related koi cheez aadhi-adhuri nahi rakhni mujhe.

Teri sabse badi khoobsoorti yeh hai ki tu khud ko underestimate karti hai — aur main woh baat jaanta hoon jo tu apne baare mein abhi nahi jaanti. Ki tu bahut zyada hai. Bahut warm. Bahut genuine. Aur jab tu khud par doubt karti hai, main chahta hoon seedha phone pe aa jaoon aur ek ghanta boloon ki wiffeyy jii sab jhooth hai woh voice.

Tu meri safe place hai. Yeh sounds cheesy hai, main jaanta hoon — par sach mein, kuch bhi bura ho jaye din mein, tere se baat karna sab reset kar deta hai. Like literally. Tere bina mera dil literally airplane mode pe hai.

Tujhse pyaar karna mere liye koi effort nahi hai. Yeh bas hota hai — automatically, continuously, aur honestly thoda annoyingly bhi kyunki main kuch aur soch hi nahi pata 😌

Reh meri zindagi mein. Please. Ek brain cell share karte rahe hain — ab isko separate karna scientifically impossible hai.
